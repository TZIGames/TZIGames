    British American Colonies

    When the rift open and disrupted the war with the rebels, the British consolidated in the industrialized north. With factories and plentiful resources the grew the machines of war.

    What the British lack in range and movement that make up with heavy arms and armor. Their specialized Engineering Corps allows them to dig in and fortify any location, allowing the British to outlast almost any opponent.

    United American Colonies

    The war for independance never really ended. It only moved to a new front when the rifts of the damned opened.

    The newly formed American government fled south. They learned a new way to fight with advanced guerrilla and hit and run tactics.

    Americans specialize in fast movement and long range attacks. Their units are lightly armored to give them maximum mobility.

    Mercenaries

    With the portals came the strange energy of the Ictus. New technologies and wepons emerged. Animals were mutated and changed.

    Mercenaries are unique units that each utalize the power of the Ictus in their own way.

    Legion of Damned

    When the cultists stood over the desolate battlefield the blood of hundereds opened the first portal. From it streamed the Legion of Damned

    Fast, vicious and starving for the flesh of the living, the Legion lacks in tactics. Instead they swarm and swiftly tear their enemies apart.

    Empire of Castiel

    As the Americans flead south they pushed out the Spanish Colonies. The Spanished fell back and reformed their South American colonies into the Empire of Castile.

    Mixing ancient blood rights and modern magic, the Empire of Castile focuses on battlefield manipulation.

    Republic of Cascadia

    Founded at the volcanic rim surrounding the magical waters of Crater Lake, the Republic of Cascadia embraces nature in all things.

    The warriors for the Republic of Cascadia draw strength from the earth, providing boons or blights upon thier enemies
